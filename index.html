ABOUT
OUR COMPANY

NICE AND PURE FOODS PVT LTD.

Nice & Pure Foods PVT LTD committed to delivering premium quality rice to global markets. Since 1960, we have been proudly serving the rice industry, building a strong reputation based on trust, quality, and consistency.

With decades of experience, we specialize in sourcing, processing, and exporting high-grade rice under strict quality control and international standards. Our mission is to provide pure, hygienic, and nutritious rice products while maintaining long-term relationships with our global clients.

PRODUCTS

1121 SELLA PARBOILED RICE

High-quality parboiled rice processed through soaking, steaming, and drying. Known for its golden color, strong grains, and non-sticky texture, making it ideal for export and long storage.
